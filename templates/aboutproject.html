This project allows users to detect Indian Sign Language gestures using hand landmarks in real time. The gestures are recognized and converted into text and speech using a combination of machine learning and computer vision techniques.

ğŸš€ How It Works

  • Step 1: We capture the hand using webcam video feed.
  • Step 2: MediaPipe Hands detects 21 hand landmarks from the frame.
  • Step 3: We extract 9 Euclidean distances between selected landmark pairs to create feature vectors.
  • Step 4: A pre-trained Bayesian Classifier predicts the corresponding alphabet or word.
  • Step 5: The output is shown as text and can also be converted to speech.
